Why is it promising to work at Tipitek?

  • Transparency and openness

    This is Tipitek's philosophy. We aim to be as open as possible with both our customers and our staff. This means that your interests will always be protected. The Tipitek team is always respectful and creative.

  • Constant development

    We never stop at what has been achieved and constantly keep abreast of innovative trends in the crypto-sphere. The ongoing development of the company means constant development of each of its members, a powerful progression of skills and professional level.

  • Customer centricity

    We always work to clearly understand what our customers want. And based on that we create products, services and processes that best meet their needs. Being part of the creation of such products is a unique opportunity for every professional.

  • Financial Perspectives

    Today, working in the cryptocurrency industry is one of the highest paying jobs. We offer competitive salaries, career prospects, and therefore pay. In addition, you can get paid both in cryptocurrency and fiat money.

  • Freedom

    We are for working outside the box and boundaries, so we are more than happy to hire both full-time and remote employees. After all, the most important thing is a person's professional qualities and experience. In addition, we offer flexible working hours for each employee.

How does the hiring process work?

  • Stage 1

    You send your CV. Recommendations can be an additional benefit.

  • Stage 2

    Then you have a two-stage interview with a representative of the HR department and the department you would like to work in.

  • Stage 3

    If the interview is successful, we send and coordinate with you our job offer.

  • Stage 4

    The last stage is the actual contractual formalization of our employment relationship. After that you become a full member of the Tipitek team!
